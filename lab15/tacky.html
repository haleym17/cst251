Purpose

The purpose of this lab is to stylize our site with new CSS techniques like text-shadow, gradient backgrounds, image filters, and rounded corners.

Challenges

This lab was super refreshing after the brain melting typography labs, so it actually seemed super easy and simple. I would also like to note that I have used some of these elements in previous labs because I typically scroll through w3schools and find new things to try. With that being said, text shadows, image filters and transforms are the ones I have used before. So yes, overall this lab was not challenging at all and everything ran smoothly.

Results

If everything came out as planned, then my site should look a more stylized than normal! My images should be filtered, my corners rounded, my background a gradient, and my text and boxes shadowed.
